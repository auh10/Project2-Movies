Welcome

    If you're curious about highly rated movie choices, you have come to the right place. We have created this site to showcase our list of top movies between the years 2000 through 2020. You will be introduced to a variety of movie genres and we will explore any correlations or relationships between various aspects of these movies. For example:

      Do high ratings mean high-grossing movies?

      Is there a specific genre that often produces box-office hits?

      Does the release year have any affect on the ratings?

      Was there a year that a specific genre was popular?

      Does the director influence movie ratings?

    On this website, we will be exploring some of our curious questions and sharing our findings. The relationships or lack of relationships will be displayed on the Visualizations link in the form of graphs. Thanks for visiting our site, and hope you enjoy our movie picks.
